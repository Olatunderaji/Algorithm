CHECKPOINT
PROBLEM
Algorithm that read a sentence, which ends with a point, character by character, and to determine:
1. Word Count
2. Vowel Count
SOLUTION:
-Create a Variable to hold
    Sentence, Wordcount, VowelChar, VowelCount
-Continue counting until getting to point
-Read VowelChar (a'e'i'o'u)
-Read Sentence Char by Char
-If Sentence Char match any VowelChar
-Add 1 to VowelCount
